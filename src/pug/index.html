Teaching Philosophy

I believe that children deserve an education that is tailored to each of their needs as much as possible. I understand that every student is unique and learns in different ways and at different paces. Therefore, I make it my goal to meet students where they are and lead them towards success in every aspect of their education. I endeavor to create an atmosphere where children are comfortable to ask questions, unafraid to make mistakes, eager to discover, and challenged to go above and beyond their goals and expectations. It is my hope that my students will never want to stop learning!
